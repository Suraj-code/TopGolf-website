RULES OF PLAY

rules

There's a 6-shot limit per hole so you don't hold up the next group. Not holed the ball after 6 shots? Give yourself a score of 7 and move on.

Let your whole gang tee off before anyone plays a second shot.

Golfing groups should be 4 or less.

Love your club - no sharing.

Stuck? Move your ball up to 4 inches (one putter head) from an obstacle

Ball gone walkabout? Drop it where it left the course and take a 1- shot penalty.

Move on to the next hole as soon as you've finished.

KEEPING SCORE
Just count the number of shots it takes you to get the ball in the hole. Whoever has the lowest score wins the game.
